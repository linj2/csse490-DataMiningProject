Violation Distrubution By Borough

While it is interesting to see the trends of the various violations that occur across the entire state of New York, what's even more intriguing is seeing how the violations are distributed within each individual borough.
The bar chart below depicts the number of violations that occur per borough. What is even more intriguing is the fact that if you were to go based on population from smallest to largest, it would go Staten Island, Bronx,
Manhattan, Queens, and then Brooklyn. However, Brooklyn's violation count almost matches that of Queens, yet both Queens and Brooklyn have significantly less violations that occur in them than Manhattan.
